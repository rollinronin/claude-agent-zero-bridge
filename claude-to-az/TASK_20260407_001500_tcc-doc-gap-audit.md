# TCC IS Portfolio Documentation Gap Audit

## Context

Rollin is Senior PM for Infrastructure Services at the Transit Control Center (TCC), operated by Metro Transit at the Twin Cities Metropolitan Council. He manages three IS projects that share construction dependencies and a common SharePoint-based data architecture. We are in Phase 1 of standing up the portfolio management system, and the documentation gap audit is a critical early step before importing the ~914-task WBS into SharePoint Lists.

## The Three IS Projects

### 1. TCC Expansion (EXP)
- IS lead role
- PC refresh for 30 operator consoles: 2 IS PCs each (Council network + MTPD network) = 60 IS PCs total
- Solacom PCs managed by IES, not IS
- Construction-dependent schedule; Notice to Proceed pending
- ~914 tasks in full WBS
- Working assumption: construction September 2026, avoiding MN State Fair in August
- Temporary space: EBC Room 1007 during construction

### 2. Solacom NG911 Refresh (NG911)
- IS coordination role (FE leads RFP)
- Goal: new vendor owns 100% end-to-end support, eliminating 4 dedicated Mitel lines
- IS Senior SME (former TCC/Network team manager) is Council's primary technical rep

### 3. VuWall (VUW)
- IS coordination role
- Video wall/KVM proof of concept
- Non-refundable POC cost is primary risk
- PO path and IS resource for POC support still open from steering committee
